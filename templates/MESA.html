MESA CLUB

MESA (Mechanical Engineering Students’ Association) is one of the vibrant student organizations at K. K. Wagh College of Engineering & Research, Nashik, under the Department of Mechanical Engineering. It is dedicated to enriching students' academic and professional journey by organizing technical workshops, competitions, industrial visits, and hands-on sessions. The club fosters skills in areas like thermodynamics, CAD modeling, machine design, and manufacturing processes, preparing students for the real-world challenges of engineering industries.
