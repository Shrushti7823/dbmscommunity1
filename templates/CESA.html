CESA CLUB

CESA (Computer Engineering Students' Association) is one of the most vibrant technical clubs of K. K. Wagh College of Engineering & Research. It focuses on promoting technical excellence and leadership among students through seminars, coding competitions, workshops, and industrial visits. The club aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by nurturing students in programming, networking, and system design.
