CHEMFEST

CHEMFEST is the signature technical and cultural event of the Chemical Engineering Department at K. K. Wagh College of Engineering & Research. It aims to promote awareness and practical skills in areas such as chemical process engineering, sustainability, environmental safety, and new-age materials. Students actively engage in organizing technical paper presentations, project expos, quizzes, workshops, and fun science-based games that spark innovation and collaboration across departments.
