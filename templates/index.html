Community Management System

The Community Management System is a centralized platform designed to efficiently manage and organize data related to various committees within the institute. The system allows administrators to maintain comprehensive records of each committee, including detailed descriptions, associated photographs, and the list of faculty members involved. Faculty profiles within the system capture essential information such as names, designations, qualifications, and contact details.
