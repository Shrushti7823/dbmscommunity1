DESOC - Design Society of Computer Science & Design
Where Creativity Meets Code.

Founded in 2024, DESOC is the hub for innovation at the intersection of Computer Science and Design. We empower students to combine creativity with technology through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions. Our mission is to foster a community where coding and design come together to solve real-world problems.
